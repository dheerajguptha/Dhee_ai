## Phase
Phase 1 — Product.

## Goal
Define what Dhee_AI is, for whom, and how success is measured, and enumerate the full capability
set that the architecture and milestones must satisfy.

## Scope
- Product vision, mission, personas, non-goals, competitive analysis.
- Master PRD: goals, requirements per phase, acceptance criteria.
- Feature matrix across all capability categories.

## Deliverables
- `PRODUCT_VISION.md`, `MASTER_PRD.md`, `FEATURE_MATRIX.md`, `ROADMAP.md`.

## Acceptance criteria
- Every capability in scope appears in `FEATURE_MATRIX.md` with status, phase, priority,
  dependencies, and planned version.
- PRD requirements trace to roadmap phases and feature-matrix entries.
- Documents pass Repository Validation (links, naming, precedence).

## Dependencies
- MILESTONE_01 (constitution and governance in place).

## Risks & mitigations
- **Risk:** scope creep. **Mitigation:** explicit non-goals in `PRODUCT_VISION.md` and phase
  gating in `ROADMAP.md`.
